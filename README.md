# Portfolio Site

Hello! My name is Buck Harris. If you are viewing this, then you are probably also viewing the site that I have created. This is my first React-based project that I have worked on since the [PHaSTphoto](https://github.com/SeniorDesign2023/PHaSTphoto) project that a team and I worked on for our senior assignment at the University of Wyoming.

I am by no means the fastest, most competent, or most amazing programmer in the world, but I do think that this site showcases my ability to jump into a new objective and problem-solve a solution.

My first crack at this site produced something that worked, was deliverable, and probably acceptable to someone. However, it was something that I was not proud of, as I relied on too many AI tools and skipped the learning process of this framework. Because of that, I basically did an in-place rewrite of all the code to make it my own work. I was surprised to see how much bloat was produced in the first pass, and I was embarrassed to have even pushed and published it to begin with. AI is cool and will have its place, but simplicity almost always wins. You can’t expect to get something refined by adding unnecessary junk to it for the sake of it.
Although probably trivial to most of you, something that I am very proud of is the way this code is organized. To me, it feels very readable and portable for future projects.

Thank you for viewing my work, and I hope that maybe one day I can be an asset, even if it’s just to one person.
